## Post Title

## Post Content

Hey Indie Hackers! 👋

I've been working on something that solves a real pain point I faced: getting reliable SaaS pricing data is expensive and time-consuming.

## The Problem

When I was building pricing tools for SaaS companies, I needed competitive intelligence data. The existing solutions cost $1K+/year, and scraping pricing pages manually was a nightmare.

## What I Built

**SaaS Pricing Intelligence API** - A simple API that gives you pricing data for 500+ SaaS products.

**Live URL**: http://ec2-54-68-138-190.us-west-2.compute.amazonaws.com:5000

### Features:
- **500+ products** tracked
- **Real-time pricing** data
- **Historical trends** for tracking changes over time
- **Product comparison** endpoints
- **Category-based search**

### Pricing:
- **Free**: 100 API calls/day (perfect for testing)
- **Startup**: $49/mo - 5,000 calls/day
- **Business**: $199/mo - 50,000 calls/day
- **Enterprise**: $499/mo - unlimited

## Why This Matters

The SaaS market is $247B+ and growing. Companies need competitive intelligence to:
- Price their products competitively
- Understand market positioning
- Identify upsell opportunities
- Build sales enablement tools

The data moat gets more valuable over time—historical pricing data is a real competitive advantage.
